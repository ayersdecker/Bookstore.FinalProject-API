About Us

Tiger Book Exchange is a platform that allows students to buy and sell their textbooks to one another. Our goal is to make it easy for students to save money on textbooks and to help reduce waste by promoting the reuse of textbooks.

Our Services

  • Store your profile information
  • Store book information, including author, name, edition, description, ISBN, associated courses, and book condition
  • Keep track of the seller and buyer for each book
  • Keep track of all interested patrons of a book and the winning patron
  • Handle payment transactions through a third-party provider
  • Filter books based on author, title, ISBN, and courses
  • Allow sellers to remove their books from the database
  • Allow interested patrons to withdraw their interest in a book
  • Display a history of all books a seller has posted and all books a buyer has bought
  • Allow sellers to modify their books after posting them
  • Store images of book condition in cloud storage
